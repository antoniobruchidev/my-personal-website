About me

Hi, I'm Antonio Bruchi, 41 at the time of writing and for the most of my life I've been cheffing. Now I'm currently requalifying as Full Stack Developer and I hope... no, I'm sure I can be an excellent one!

Key Life and Work experiences

I'm Italian, born and bred. When I was 14 I decided I wanted to have one year of study abroad so I applied trought AFS, passed many psycologycal evaluations and by the time I was 16 I was accepted and had my year abroad in California. So wonderful, yet hard experience, it'd take a book to tell the tale.

Back in Italy I finished secondary school and started working, a bit as computer repairer, a bit as a chef. This until my early twenties, when after a couple of workplaces I opened a restaurant and run it for two long years. Tired of the very long hours (about 16 per day for 400 in a row), I decided I had to step down and put it up for sale.

After the well deserved rest I tried an experience in Austria, but honestly it wasn't that great. When my brother told me I was going to become an uncle, I felt I had to come to U.K to reunite the family. So I did and I've been cheffing in Cardiff since 2015. That is until an year ago when I felt I needed to change. I'm a Code Insitute scholar training to be a Full Stack Developer.
